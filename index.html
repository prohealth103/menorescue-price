According to breakthrough research, it has been found that every menopausal woman exhibits unhealthy levels of cortisol, which is responsible for the body's response to stress. These abnormal cortisol levels have been identified as the primary reason why women experience physical, psychological, and cognitive health deterioration after menopause.
